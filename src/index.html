You must enable the "Access your data for all websites" permission to use this extension.
  • Navigate to the Add-ons Manager page (about:addons).
  • Click the "Search All Tabs (Simple but Fast)" extension item.
  • Click the "Permissions" tab on the right side of the "Details" tab.
  • Toggle on the "Access your data for all websites" permission.
  • Refresh this page.
Press Escape or Spacebar to focus the search box.
Press Enter to perform search.
Press Ctrl-Enter to perform case-sensitive search.
